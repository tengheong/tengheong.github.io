Techniques

GIS for hillshade and landcover analysis, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop

The route has three phases: each phase consists of 7 days of biking and the 8th day as the rest day. In total, it takes 23 days to complete the 2149-mile route. This bike tour will bring you to famous attractions in Southeast Asia.

How to find your ideal neighborhood using GIS?


Techniques

Esri ArcMap


My client is moving to Madison and is looking for an ideal neighborhood with the following criteria:

  1. Within 4 miles from workplace (Music Hall)
  2. Within 1 mile from local natural attraction such as Lake Mendota, Lake Monona, and UW Arboretum.
  3. 0.25 miles away from Superfund sites
  4. Within local school districts

Walkability Analysis for Truax Air Park Zones


Techniques

Esri ArcMap


Dane County Regional Airport is exploring potential development opportunities around the area surrounding the airport (Truax Air Park Zones). The area includes Madison Area Technical School (MATC) which has over thousands of students, faculties and staff. Before making any recommendation, I did a transporation analysis on the walkability to existing restaurants and bus station from the Truax Air Park Zones. The analysis reveals whether the exisiting public transportation and restaurants amenities meet the demands of the population.
